Welcome to the Chair of Uncertainty Quantification and Statistical Learning at the Research Center Trustworthy Data Science and Security (UA Ruhr) and the Department of Statistics (Technische Universität Dortmund). In addition to teaching graduate courses on Bayesian statistics and machine learning, we are involved in several research projects.

Feel free to follow us on Twitter!
